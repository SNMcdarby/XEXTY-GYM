Kitenge

What is a kitenge

Kitenge are colourful pieces of fabric. Found in the Coastal area of Kenya, and in Tanzania,decorated with variety of colors, patterns. With swahili sayings written on them including political slogans.

Artwork

Most designs have fancy prints with colorful or detailed on the obverse side known as traditional tribal patterns.

African Modern Fashion Design

These fabrics are now used in Modern african and fashion clothing designs all around the world.

About

Kitenge derives from a swahili. Kitenge is an african fabric found in East Africa known as Chitenge, and the West Africa and Central African it is known as Ankara.
